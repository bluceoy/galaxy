**What ARG-Annotation Does**

To quickly survey ARGs profile in metagenomic dataset, we published a pipeline combine the speed of Usearch and the accuracy of blastx. We have constructed an online pipeline with Galaxy. Due to large size of metagenomics data, users are suggested to firstly do quick search in local computer with usearch and then submit the potential fasta sequences for accurate ARG type identification.

Download the stage one program: https://github.com/biofuture/Ublastx_stageone
